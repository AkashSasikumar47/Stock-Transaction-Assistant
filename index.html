About Us

IEEE SRM Student Branch Power Electronics Society

Welcome to our dynamic community, where we focus on the effective use of electronic components and the development of analytical tools for efficient power conversion, control, and conditioning.

Our mission is to advance the theory and practice of electrical and electronics engineering, with a specific focus on the development and application of power electronic systems and technologies.

Since 1990

With over 35 years of experience, we've been at the forefront of guiding the development and innovation in power electronics technology.

1000+ members

Join our community and be a part of a dynamic society shaping the future of power electronics technology.
